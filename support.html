GrowMe Support and FAQ

Help! How do I get started?

Press the '+' icon in the top left corner of your screen and follow the steps to add a new plant.

What do the different symbols mean?

  • The water droplet without any a checkmark indicates that your plant should be watered on that day.
  • The water droplet without a checkmark indicates that the plant has been watered for the day. To set a day as complete, press a water droplet without a checkmark on the main plants list screen or in the edit plant view.
  • The checkmark without a droplet indicates that a plant does not need to be watered for that day.

How do I let the app know that I watered a plant for a given day?

To set a day as complete, press a water droplet without a checkmark on the main plants list screen or in the edit plant view. The water droplet will change to a droplet with a checkmark to indicate that the app knows your plant has been watered for that day.

How do I remove a plant?

On the main screen (plant list view) press down on a plant and swipe to the left. A delete option so appear to the right of the plant's cell; press that button and the plant will be removed.

How can I change any of the settings of my plant?

Press on the plant and you will be taken to the edit screen. From there press on any of the given choices and then press 'Save' in the upper right hand corner for the changes to take affect. If you don't want to keep your changes press 'Cancel' in the upper left corner.

How can I stop receiving notifications for a plant?

Press on the plant and you will be taken to the edit screen. From there scroll down and switch the 'Notifications' switch to off.
